Common Questions

How do I cancel my subscription? expand_more

You can manage your subscription directly through your Apple ID settings. Go to Settings > Subscription Management to view options.

Can I sync with Apple Health? expand_more

Yes, absolutely. Navigate to Settings > Integrations and toggle the Apple Health switch to enable seamless data syncing.

How do I reset my workout streaks? expand_more

Streak resets must be handled by our support team to prevent accidental data loss. Please use the 'Contact Support' option below.
